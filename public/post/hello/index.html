問1

$$ A{\Delta}B = (A{\backslash}B){\cup}(B{\backslash}A) \\\
= (A{\cap}B^c){\cup}(B{\cap}A^c) \\\
= ((A{\cap}B^c){\cup}B){\cap}((A{\cap}B^c){\cup}A^c) \\\
= (A{\cup}B){\cap}(A^c{\cup}B^c) \\\
= (A{\cup}B){\cap}(A{\cap}B)^c \\\
=(A{\cup}B){\backslash}(A{\cap}B) \\\
$$
